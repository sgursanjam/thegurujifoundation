About Guruji Foundation

We Are a NGO

Guruji Foundation is non profit organisation whose primary aim is to serve humanity. With more than 100branches in around 20 different countries we carry out various humanitarian works wherever needed.


We have succesfully funded education of more than 10000 children from across the world till now. We have also succesfully managed to fund the treatment of more than 1million people till now.
We have carried out marriages of more than 10000couples from financially weak sections who wished to spend their lives together.

Our Work

Education

We provide education facilities in remote parts of the world where people are not financially stable to afford for education

Healthcare

We regularly conduct health camps, blood camps, checkups in slum areas of world where health facilties are a dream for people.

Marriages

Every year we help thousands of poor couples to get married and live a happy life and we make sure marriage function is never a burden on them

Jobs and Business

Every month we help thousands of people to get jobs or help them in setting up their small businesses to make them financially strong.

Disaster Relief

Whether it is an earthquake, flood, drought or landslide, our teams are always present at the sites to help people.

Refugees

We provide food and shelter to the who already have suffered a lot are made to suffer more in refugee camps in different areas of the world.

Mission Delhi Slums 2020

At a time when whole world was struggling to fight the covid virus, our teams identified more than 50 different slums in delhi where even basic amennities like food and health were missing. We provided food and medicines in all of these areas for more than 3months till the government was able to arrange a permanent solution for them

Some of our past Works

From last few years we have tried reaching almost all parts of the world to help needy ones.
Here are some of the glimpses of our work.

We were at Indonesia
  • January 1, 2020

In January 2020, there were widespread floods in Jakarta, Indonesia killing around 20people. Our teams reached there and helped people with food and medicines. We also helped rebuild damaged buildings

We were at Turkey
  • January 24, 2020

An earthquake of magnitutde 6.8 hit eastern Turkey killing 41 people. We helped in search operations for missing people and helped people with food and medicines

We were at Philippines
  • November 13, 2020

Typhoon Vamco hit Manila and killed around 42 people. Another typhoon Goni followed making the situation worse. Our teams reached immediately to carry out relief works
